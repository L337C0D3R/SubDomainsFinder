When you perform a subdomain search using our service, we collect and store the fetched subdomain data in our database. We also employ Cloudflare caching services to temporarily store data, which enhances website performance and user experience.

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
